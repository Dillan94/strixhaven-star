Mystery Muffin Menace Raises Campus Safety Concerns

By Mina Lee, Investigative Reporter

As students and faculty return from winter break to resume their studies, a troubling development has emerged that has campus security questioning food safety protocols. Unidentified confections bearing identical "secret admirer" messages have appeared across Strixhaven's campus, raising serious concerns about magical food security in our academic community.

The investigation began when Greta Gorunn, a first-year Lorehold student and member of the Iron-Lifters Society, discovered a suspicious muffin near Strixhaven Stadium during her morning workout routine. The baked good came wrapped in a bag with a handwritten note claiming to be from a "secret admirer."

"I was excited at first," Gorunn admitted during an exclusive interview. "Who doesn't want a secret admirer? But then I found out other students got the exact same thing, word for word."

Further investigation revealed multiple students across different colleges discovered identical muffins and notes, all bearing the same unsophisticated handwriting. Analysis conducted by this reporter confirms the messages were written by a single individual, ruling out any coordinated effort by multiple students.

Perhaps most concerning is the casual attitude some students have taken toward consuming unidentified magical items. Dream, a first year student who assisted in the investigation, expressed alarm at this trend.

"Students should verify food is safe before eating anything found unattended - magical or otherwise," Dream emphasized. "Some have found muffins on tables and benches around campus. Do none of you have any caution? Have none of you been sick?"

When asked about their own approach to mysterious food discoveries, students showed varying levels of caution. Some indicated they would discard suspicious items, while others admitted they might investigate the source of anonymous gifts.

The incident has prompted calls for increased awareness about magical food safety. Students who discover any suspicious items are urged to report them to faculty immediately rather than attempting consumption.

As of press time, faculty members have not responded to requests for comment about enhanced security measures or investigation protocols. This reporter will continue following this developing story.
